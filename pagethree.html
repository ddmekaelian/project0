Ecosystem

Coconut crabs are found on various islands near the Indian and Pacific Ocean, living in warm weather.

Australia and Madagascar are the main places that Coconut Crabs are found – they are found on the Eastern side of the Pacific Ocean as well, including (but not limited to) the Gambier Islands and Pitcairn Islands.

Below are different types of Coconut Crabs found in various parts of the world:

  • Australia & Madagascar
  • Gambier Islands & Pitcairn Islands
  • New Zealand
  • Cook Islands – near Hawaii
